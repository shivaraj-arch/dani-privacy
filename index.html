Dani — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 5 July 2026 · Applies to the Dani Android app (com.dani.translator)

Dani is a real-time voice translation app. This policy explains what personal data we handle, why, the legal basis, who we share it with, and the rights you have — including under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and the Information Technology Rules. For the purposes of the DPDP Act, the operator of Dani is the Data Fiduciary.

1. Information we collect

DataPurpose
Email address & display nameCreate and sign in to your account
Contacts you add inside DaniLet you call people you choose (we never read your phone's address book)
Online / last-seen presenceShow your Dani contacts whether you're available
Preferred languageSet your default translation language
Device push token (FCM)Ring your device for incoming calls, even when the app is closed
Microphone audio (during a call/Solo session only)Produce the live translation and transcript
Blocks & reports you submitSafety — stop unwanted contact and let you report abuse

2. Legal basis

We process your personal data on the basis of your consent, which you give when you create an account and agree to this policy and our Terms of Service. You may withdraw consent at any time (see Your rights), though some features will stop working without the data they need.

3. Voice audio and transcripts

  • During a call or Solo session your microphone audio is streamed in real time to our translation service, which uses Google's Gemini API to produce the translated speech and transcript. Audio is processed transiently to deliver the translation; we do not retain recordings of your calls on our servers after the call ends.
  • Transcripts are shown live during the call. If you choose to download a transcript, the file is saved locally on your device and is under your control.

4. Who we share data with (processors)

We do not sell your data or show ads. We use the following service providers to operate Dani:

  • Supabase — authentication, account data, contacts, presence, blocks/reports.
  • LiveKit Cloud — real-time audio transport between call participants.
  • Google (Gemini API) — speech translation and transcription.
  • Google Firebase (FCM) — delivering incoming-call notifications to your device.

5. Cross-border transfer

The service providers above operate servers outside India (primarily the United States), so your data is transferred and processed outside India. We rely on your consent and the providers' own safeguards for this transfer, as permitted under the DPDP Act. We do not transfer data to any territory the Government of India has restricted.

6. Your rights (DPDP Act)

You have the right to:

  • Access a summary of the personal data we hold about you and how it is processed.
  • Correct, complete, or update your data.
  • Erase your data / delete your account — see Delete your account.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Grievance redressal — raise a complaint with our Grievance Officer (below).
  • Nominate another person to exercise your rights in the event of death or incapacity.

To exercise any right, email us at shivrajsys@gmail.com or delete your account in-app (Settings). We respond within the timelines below.

7. Data retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. When you delete your account, or when the purpose is served, we erase your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Children

Dani is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Data breach

In the event of a personal data breach, we will notify the Data Protection Board of India and affected users as required under the DPDP Act.
